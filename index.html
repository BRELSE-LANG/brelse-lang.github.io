A hybrid language of Braille + Morse for humans and machines

What is BrelseLang?

BrelseLang is a raw, physical coding language combining braille’s dots and morse’s dashes. It's designed to be touched, pulsed, or printed – used in war zones, smart fabrics, or silent messaging systems. No GUI. No voice. Just universal sensation.

Our Mission

To democratize machine interaction by making programming and communication tactile-first — accessible to all, resilient in any environment.

Our Vision

A future where code is felt, not just seen or heard. Where screenless, haptic communication empowers security and accessibility.
