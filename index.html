What is BrelseLang?

BrelseLang is a tactile-first coding and communication system that merges Braille’s structure with Morse’s rhythm. Designed for war zones, smart wearables, neuro-devices, and silent communication. No screens. No voice. Just sensation.

Cybersecurity

Accessibility

Universal input/output for all users, including visually impaired.
